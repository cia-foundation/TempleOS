$FG,5$Assembler Directives$FG$

See $LK-A,"::/TempleOS/Compiler/OpCodes.TXT"$ for opcodes.  They're not standard.  Some invalid instructions are not flagged and some valid instructions are not implemented. 16-bit asm support is limited.

Here are example instruction formats:
$HL,1$	ADD	RAX,I64 FS:DISP[RSI+RDI*8]
$HL,0$$HL,1$	ADD	RAX,I64 [DISP]
$HL,0$
